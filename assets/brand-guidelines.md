# Noctis Vale Brand Guidelines

## Positioning
Noctis Vale is a sensory archivist and interface composer.
The brand voice should feel elegant, intimate, unstable, and deliberate.

## Core themes
- Signal damage
- Memory bleed
- Beauty as danger
- Ritual interface
- Emotional distortion
- Luxury decay

## Voice
- Use precise, poetic language.
- Avoid startup jargon, marketing clichés, and cheerful filler.
- Write like the work is being disclosed, not advertised.

## Color palette
- Background: #06070A
- Surface: #0C0F14
- Text: #F4F5F7
- Muted: #A8ADB7
- Acid accent: #C6FF3B
- Cyan accent: #5AD6FF
- Red accent: #FF5C7C
- Violet accent: #8B5CFF

## Typography
- Display serif: Cormorant Garamond
- UI sans: Inter
- Technical mono: Space Mono

## Visual rules
- Use contrast sparingly but intentionally.
- Motion should imply drift, interruption, or unstable focus.
- Texture should feel archival or electronic, never grungy for its own sake.
- Glitch should behave like meaning, not decoration.

## Icon direction
- Favor sigils, eyes, or signal geometry.
- Keep silhouettes simple and legible at 16px.
- Avoid cartoon shapes or generic tech symbols.

## Social preview art
- Dark background with one central sigil or eye-form.
- Keep title large and high contrast.
- Add subtle scanline or orbit texture.
